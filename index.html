Coffee bean flavour profile per region of growth during 2018

Each point on the map represents a region where coffee is grown on a large scale for export. Hover over a dot to better understand the regions coffee flavour profile.

Coffee export rate per country from 2014-2018

This bar chart shows the amount of coffee exported per year per location. Use the filters & sliders below the chart to view a specific year, region (some regions may not have any exporting data present) or coffee export amount.



Coffee import rate per country from 2014-2018

This bar chart shows the amount of coffee imported per year per location. Use the filters & sliders below the chart to view a specific year, region (some regions may not have any import data present) or coffee import amount.



Coffee bag retail price time series from 2014-2018 per country

Each line on this chart represents a country and there coffee bag prices. Hover over a point on the line to get a granular view of Location, Year and price. Click on a Location on the legend to highlight the location on the line chart.



Click on a country in the legend to highlight that region.

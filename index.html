Coffee bean flavour profile per region of growth during 2018

Each point on the map represents a region where coffee is grown on a large scale for export. Hover over a dot to better understand the regions coffee flavour profile. The most popular coffee blends are the Arabica and Robusta coffee blends according to the international coffee organisation Arabica coffee beans make up 60% of the worlds coffee. Arabica coffee beans tends to have a smoother, sweeter taste, with flavour notes of chocolate and sugar. They often also have hints of fruits or berries. Robusta, on the other hand, has a stronger, harsher and more bitter taste, with grainy or rubbery overtones.

Coffee export rate per country from 2014-2018

This bar chart shows the amount of coffee exported per year per location. Use the filters & sliders below the chart to view a specific year, region or greater than coffee export amount. Our chart shows that Brazil is the leader when it comes to coffee exports, shipping approximately 35 million 60KG coffee bags in 2018 alone. Brazil has been the leader for several years as their blend is creamy with low acidity which makes it a good affordable choice for cafes around the world.



Coffee import rate per country from 2014-2018

This bar chart shows the amount of coffee imported per year per location. Use the filters & sliders below the chart to view a specific year, region or greater than coffee import amount. Our chart shows that USA is the leader when it comes to coffee imports, recieving approximately 29 million 60KG coffee bags in 2018. This is most likely due to the large population and amount of coffee houses located in USA with around 35,616 present in 2018.

Coffee retail price per country during 2018

Each country on the chloropleth map that has data represents a region's coffee retail price during 2018. This view highlights which regions have more expensive coffee prices when directly compared to one another. In the United Kingdom the average price for a espresso (30 ml of coffee) is approximately $6.49 AUD when compared to Australia where the price is approximately $3.50. This is most likely due to the differences in supply and demand in these regions.
